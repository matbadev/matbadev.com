Canteens Karlsruhe & Pforzheim

This page provides general information for the Android app Canteens Karlsruhe & Pforzheim with the package name de.b_ma.canteens (in the following referred to as "app").

Availability

The app is available in Google Play Store:

Data privacy

The app does not collect, store or send any data from you or your device to a remote server.

The only remote data source that the app connects to is the Studierendenwerk Karlsruhe which is used to access the menus available in the supported canteens.
